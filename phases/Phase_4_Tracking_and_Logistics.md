# Phase 4: QR Tracking & Logistics

## Goals
-   Implement **Live Tracking** for relief supplies.
-   Use **QR Codes** to verify receipt of goods and prevent hoarding.

## Key Tasks
1.  **QR Lifecycle Management:**
    -   **NGO Side:** Generates a QR code for a "Relief Package".
    -   **Transport Side:** Scans QR when picking up.
    -   **Needy Side:** Shows a personal "ID QR" to the relief worker for verification upon delivery.
2.  **Live Status Tracking:**
    -   Step-by-step progress tracking: `Requested -> Matched -> In Transit -> Delivered`.
    -   Push notifications on the Mobile app for status updates.
3.  **Impact Analytics:**
    -   Visual dashboard showing: "1000 Blankets delivered successfully in Shimla valley today."

## Success Metrics
-   A full tracking loop: From a "Need Posted" to a "QR Scanned Delivery Successful" notification.
